PART I · WESTERN

Western Wedding Traditions

西方婚礼传统

Western weddings trace their roots to ancient Greece and Rome, later shaped by Christian doctrine.

西方婚礼起源于古希腊与古罗马，后受基督教教义深刻影响。

The ceremony is typically held in a church, symbolizing a sacred covenant between the couple and God.

仪式通常在教堂举行，象征着新人与上帝之间的神圣契约。

The bride wears a white gown, a tradition established by Queen Victoria in 1840, symbolizing purity and innocence.

新娘身着白色婚纱，这一传统始于1840年维多利亚女王，象征纯洁与无瑕。

The exchange of rings on the left hand represents eternal love, as the vein leads directly to the heart.

左手无名指交换戒指代表永恒之爱，因该处血管直通心脏。

PART I · VOWS

Traditional Western Wedding Vows

传统西方婚礼誓言

"I, [Name], take you, [Name], to be my lawfully wedded wife/husband."

「我，[姓名]，愿娶/嫁你，[姓名]，为我合法的妻子/丈夫。」

"To have and to hold from this day forward, for better, for worse."

「从今天起，拥有你、珍惜你，无论顺境或逆境。」

"For richer, for poorer, in sickness and in health."

「无论富裕或贫穷，无论健康或疾病。」

"To love and to cherish, till death do us part."

「相爱相守，直至死亡将我们分离。」

"According to God's holy ordinance, I pledge you my faith."

「遵照上帝的神圣旨意，我向你许下我的忠诚。」

PART II · CHINESE

Chinese Wedding Traditions

中式婚礼传统

Chinese weddings follow the ancient "Three Letters and Six Etiquettes" established in the Zhou Dynasty.

中式婚礼遵循周代确立的「三书六礼」古制。

The color red dominates the ceremony, symbolizing joy, prosperity, and the warding off of evil spirits.

红色贯穿整个仪式，象征喜庆、繁荣与驱邪避凶。

The bride wears a Qun Kua — a red embroidered dress with dragon and phoenix motifs representing harmony.

新娘身着裙褂——绣有龙凤图案的红色嫁衣，象征阴阳和谐。

The Tea Ceremony is central: the couple serves tea to elders, receiving blessings and red envelopes in return.

敬茶仪式是核心环节：新人向长辈敬茶，回获祝福与红包。

PART II · VOWS

Traditional Chinese Wedding Vows

中式婚礼誓言

"Today, before our parents and honored guests, I pledge to you with the Three Letters and Six Rites as my witness."

「今日高堂在座，亲友见证，我以三书六礼为凭，凤冠霞帔为证，向你立誓。」

"From this day forth, through spring and autumn, I shall shield you from wind and rain, and share every burden with you."

「此后春秋相伴，不问寒暖，必当护你周全；柴米油盐，不怨琐碎，定要与你分担。」

"I vow to honor Heaven and Earth, to revere our parents, and above all, to cherish you."

「从此敬天地，敬高堂，更敬彼此。」

"Holding your hand, I shall not betray this life; growing old with you, I shall follow you into the next."

「执子之手，当不负此生；与子偕老，必共赴来世。」

PART III · INSIGHTS

Shared Essence, Different Expressions

殊途同归：爱的不同表达

Despite apparent differences, both traditions share the same essence: marking a new chapter of life with solemn commitment.

尽管表面差异显著，两种传统本质相同：以庄严承诺开启人生新篇章。

Western weddings emphasize romance and individual choice; Chinese weddings honor heritage and family bonds.

西方婚礼强调浪漫与个人选择；中式婚礼尊崇传统与家族纽带。

In the era of globalization, many couples now blend both styles — wearing white for the ceremony and red for the banquet.

在全球化时代，许多新人融合两种风格——仪式穿白，宴席穿红。

Ultimately, every wedding is a unique celebration of love that transcends cultural boundaries.

归根结底，每场婚礼都是超越文化边界的独特爱之庆典。
